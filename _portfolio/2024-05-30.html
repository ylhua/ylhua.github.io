VR Training Simulation for Airplane Parking

Tools: Unity, Blender, OpenXR, Meta Quest 3
Course: Virtual Reality in Theory and Practice (MAMF45), Lund University

Step into the future of ground crew training with the VR Training Simulation for Airplane Parking. This immersive simulation is designed to train staff in a realistic, hands-on virtual environment that mirrors the complexity of real-world aircraft docking.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Distance Detection: Integrated with an ABD sensor, the simulation accurately tracks the distance between the aircraft and the jetbridge. A control panel allows users to start and stop the docking process, ensuring precise alignment every time.
  • Interactive Obstacle Challenge: A movable suitcase acts as an unexpected runway obstacle, testing users’ situational awareness and reaction time. Stay alert—keeping the path clear is part of the job.
  • Immersive, Real-World Environment: Experience a life-sized apron area with realistic physics and visuals. The accurately modeled environment and ABD sensor replicate the conditions of an actual airport runway.
